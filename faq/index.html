Frequently Asked Questions

What is therapy?

Therapy is a collaborative process through which a therapist works with individuals, couples, families, or other groups of people in order to help them address problems, and to lead more satisfying and personally meaningful lives. Therapists work from a variety of perspectives, but therapy generally involves talking about issues going on in your life, with the goal of increased knowledge, awareness, and acceptance.
