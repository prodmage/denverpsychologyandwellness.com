Dr. John Bodycombe

Licensed Psychologist Candidate in Denver, CO

Dr. Bodycombe has a Doctorate in Clinical Psychology from the University of Denver - GSPP, and is a Licensed Psychologist Candidate in Colorado who works as a primary individual therapist in Denver, Colorado. He has five years of clinical experience and during that time has worked worked with people in a variety of settings, and with a wide range of issues. Dr. Bodycombe specializes in treatment of self-esteem, anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship issues and men's issues.
