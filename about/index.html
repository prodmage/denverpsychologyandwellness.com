Dr. John Bodycombe

Licensed Psychologist in Denver, CO

Dr. Bodycombe has a Doctorate in Clinical Psychology from the University of Denver - GSPP, and is a Licensed Psychologist in Colorado who works as a primary individual therapist in Denver, Colorado. He has six years of clinical experience and during that time has worked with people in a variety of settings including university counseling centers, community non-profits, post-secondary support organizations, and corrections facilities. Dr. Bodycombe specializes in identity exploration, self-esteem, relational and interpersonal difficulties, depression, trauma, and men's issues.
