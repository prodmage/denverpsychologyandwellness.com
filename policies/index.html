Policies

What does a therapy session cost?

One of the most important factors in beginning therapy is the "match" between you and your therapist. Because of this, I offer a free 50-minute initial consulation session. This is a chance for you to determine whether I am someone who you feel comfortable talking to and will be able to help you. At the same time, I will have the opportunity to learn more about the problems your are facing, and to determine if I am the best person to help.

After this initial consultation, my regular fee for a 50-minute therapy session is $150. I believe that finances alone should not be a barrier to receiving therapy however, and I am sometimes able to offer a sliding scale to clients that do not have the financial resources to pay my full fee. Please call me to learn more about this option.

Will insurance pay for my therapy?

I do not accept insurance but my fee can sometimes be reimbursed if you have a plan that covers out-of-network providers. I am happy to prepare a monthly or weekly statement for you to submit to your insurance provider.

Cancellation Policy

I request that you notify me at least 48-hours in advance if you need to cancel a scheduled therapy session. This is because when I reserve a time for you, I make that time unavailable to my other clients. I charge the full session fee if you fail to give me the required 48-hour notice.
